Visual Guide

Your Wedding Planning Timeline

Know exactly when to do what. This timeline is based on expert advice from The Wedding Police Podcast.

12 Months Before

Set the Foundation

Set your budget, start your guest list, and book your venue. These decisions shape everything else.

10 Months Before

Lock In Key Vendors

Book photographer, videographer, DJ/band, and caterer. The best vendors book up fast!

8 Months Before

Wedding Attire

Start gown shopping and formalwear fittings. As Penny says: "Don't bring an entourage to the bridal salon!"

6 Months Before

Details & Design

Choose your wedding theme, colors, invitations, and floral design. Book your officiant.

4 Months Before

Send Invitations

Mail invitations, finalize the ceremony details, plan the rehearsal dinner, and register for gifts.

2 Months Before

Final Fittings & RSVPs

Final dress fitting, confirm headcount, finalize seating chart, and write vows.

1 Month Before

Final Countdown

Confirm all vendors, break in your shoes, compile the day-of timeline, and finalize your playlist.

Wedding Week

The Big Day Prep

Rehearsal, delegate responsibilities, prepare emergency kit, and — most importantly — breathe! 💍
